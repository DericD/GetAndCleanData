##Activity Lables
* 1 - Walking
* 2 - WALKING UPSTAIRS
* 3 - WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
* 4 - SITTING
* 5 - STANDING
* 6 - LAYING

All the variable are either a Mean value or a standard deviation

* mean(): Mean value
* std(): Standard deviation
